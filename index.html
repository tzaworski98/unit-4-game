Chrystal Collector!

The computer will generate a random number for you to reach at the beginning of the game.

The four chrystals below will generate a random number as well.

When you click on one you will be able to see the number generated and it will be added to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number generated by the computer, and lose by going over that number.

When the game ends the numbers will be reset, and the values of the chrystals will change.
